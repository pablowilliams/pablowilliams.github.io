üî¨ In Development

Earnings Call Sentiment Analyzer

I have been fascinated by how much information hides in plain sight during earnings calls. When a CFO subtly hedges their language or a CEO shifts tone mid-sentence, markets often miss these signals entirely. I am building a system to catch what traditional models overlook.

Right now I am working through 18,755 earnings transcripts from the Motley Fool corpus, building out the preprocessing pipeline to segment speaker turns and strip boilerplate. The next step is fine-tuning FinBERT to detect tone shifts between quarters and quantify hedging patterns in management commentary.

The end goal is a backtested signal that can predict abnormal returns in the days following an earnings call. If the methodology holds up, I will package it as a REST API with full documentation.
